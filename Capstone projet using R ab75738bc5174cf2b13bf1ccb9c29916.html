Now we can look back for questions that we have asked before analysis:

  1. How do customers and subscribers use Cyclistic bikes differently?

Subscribers mostly use the bike-share program on weekdays, their demand for renting bikes rises throughout the summer (401906 rides in August) and falls during the winter (99517 rides in February).

Customers, on the other hand, prefer to ride on weekends, and like subscribers their demand rises throughout the summer (128634 rides in August) and falls during the winter (3396 rides in February), but the difference is much more bigger: - 75% for subscribers and -97% for customers.

  1. Why would customers riders buy Cyclistic annual memberships?

Customers have higher average trip duration (27.9 min. compare to subscriber‚Äôs 9.55 min.) which verifies the claim that customers are tourists who uses bikes for a longer duration of time.

Bike share program manager needs to provide offers on weekends trips to increase duration of trip time.

I will recommend to collaborate with hotels, or build more sharing stations near them (in case they already have sharing stations near public/culture and other popular places).

  1. How can Cyclistic use digital media to influence customers to become members?

Cyclistic can start an advertising company on annual membership during peak months from July - September

Provide a membership for weekday rentals.
